1.Abstract

SoN is a web application that helps the user find it's friends across multiple web pages and advanced search functionalilties for said webpages and show the social graph

2.Guide

Home

Details about our project.

LogIn

Login to your Github, Last.fm or Twitter.

Note: The Last.fm API only returns a given username's friends list. No login reqired.

Recommandations

Suggested friends for your logged in accounts.

Search

Select the social network you want to search in and the Username you would like to find. You can also search through all networks.

Graph

Visualize your social graph for your current accounts.
